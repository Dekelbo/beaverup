I am currently building the backend for a personal language-learning project, focusing on architecting a scalable infrastructure, robust user management, and optimized HTTP request flows. My vision is to integrate advanced AI APIs to specifically enhance speech fluency using unique methods I developed through my own long term experience learning Spanish and German, as well as teaching Hebrew. To validate these ideas, I first created an initial prototype using OpenAI’s custom GPT platform to test the teaching methods in a raw, functional format. (https://chatgpt.com/g/g-69a57b0dd5448191a560fa412e59fd3f-beaverup)

 After testing it with my Hebrew students and refining the logic based on my notes and their feedback, they saw clear benefits in the AI driven approach, confirming the effectiveness of these methods. My design vision is to house these technical capabilities within a warm, cozy minimalist and aesthetic interface, ensuring the advanced AI features live in an environment that feels inviting and stress free.
